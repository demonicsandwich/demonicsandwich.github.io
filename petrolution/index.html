Petrolution

The future of petrol

What is the problem?

Global warming and greenhouse gasses are a major modern problem. Car emission is one of the major causes of this. 28% of all gas emission is vehicle exhaust, that is 1,549 million metric tonnes of C02 from the US alone! This an extremely important problem that could eventually have disastrous consequences.

Learn more

What is our solution?

Petrolution. Using the perks of an electric car, the speed of a petrol car, and unheard of levels of efficiency, Petrolution is an innovation beyond what was ever seen before. The CO2 and H2O created through the ignition of petrol are separated and fused to be reused as fuel. The energy required to complete this task is significantly less than powering the motor of an electric car. Higher end Petrolution cars will also be equipped with solar panels to even further increase power efficiency.
