Combat Go: Best game of the year

Published November 9, 2019

"Battle and compete against formidable enemies with Combat Go. This Roman gladiator-inspired game will take you back to ancient times as you strategically work to knock your opponent down in a fight for freedom. With Combat Go, you are an Emperor of Rome. You own the gladiators, who are controlled from under the board. Your gladiators compete in a rousing game, each attempting to knock the other down. You can collect armour and legendary gladiators to aid your quest to become Rome’s most powerful Emperor." - Combat Go

Combat Go is a game of strategy, where you can learn to combine gladiators, armour, and obstacles to give you the best advantage against your competitors. Children will learn to think critically and use logic to approach challenges.

The initial starter pack comes with a battle arena, a basic gladiator, 2 handles to maneuver gladiators, a piece of armour, an obstacle, and a user’s guide with instructions and a checklist for all characters, obstacles and armour to explain all you need to know to successfully play Combat Go. Mystery Boxes that contain additional accessories that can enhance in-game performance in this thrilling game are also available for purchase.

Combat Go is an exhilarating game that will have you sitting on the edge of your seat, with constant action and suspense. Combat Go is truly a game for all ages. I had loads of fun playing it with my 10-year-old son, and so did he. He plays it with his friends for countless hours and has participated in the "Rome’s greatest Emperor" tournament. Combat Go would make a great gift for this upcoming Christmas. Two kids? No problem! With Combat go, you don't have to worry about them nagging you to take them to McDonald's or getting them the new Pokemon game. Only one kid? Just have them invite a friend over! Not to mention, for when the kids aren't using it, you have a great party game to play with friends as well! I have had a few friends over after dark for game night multiple times after buying Combat Go, and it's been a great experience every single time. Both myself, my son, as well as my wife and friends, have all made priceless memories with this game. With a great build quality, unlimited replayability, and an incredibly low price, you'd be crazy to turn this one down! Rest assured, you will certainly have a blast playing Combat Go!
